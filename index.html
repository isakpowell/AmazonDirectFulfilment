Monthly workflow: export PAID Direct Fulfilment invoices from Amazon Vendor Central for the month → drop the CSV here → review the summary (non-PAID rows are flagged and excluded by default) → download the CSV or copy it → paste directly into your Wiise Sales Order lines, one row per SKU (use the ex GST value; GST is applied via tax code). Everything runs in your browser — no data leaves this page.

Reconciling to Amazon's payment: "Total Value (inc GST)" should match the sum of "Amount in Payment Currency" for this batch in Amazon's separate Payments export (allow a few cents of rounding across many lines). Note that Amazon's "Item Cost" column is already the extended line amount — not a per-unit price — so this tool does not multiply it by Quantity.
